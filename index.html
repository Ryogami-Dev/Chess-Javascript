How to Play

  • Click on a piece to select it.
  • Click on a valid square to move it.
  • Red pieces move forward diagonally.
  • Gray pieces move backward diagonally.
  • Capture opponent's pieces by jumping over them.
  • Reach the opposite side to crown your piece as a king.
  • Kings can move in both directions.
  • Capture all opponent's pieces or block their moves to win!
