How to Play

  • Click On a piece to select it.
  • Click on a valid square to move it.
  • Red Pieces move forwards diagonally
  • Gray Pieces move backward diagonally.
  • Capture opponent's pieces by jumping over them (The piece will move 2 boxes diagonally)
  • Reach the Opposite side's first coloum to crown your piece as a king.
  • King can move in both directions backwards and forwards.
  • Caputure all opponent's pieces or block theri moves to win!
